# Quollix Trademarks and Branding

This policy explains when you may use the Quollix name, logo, website branding, and other branding assets.

## Permitted Use

You may use the Quollix name to truthfully refer to the project, for example to say that you use Quollix, provide compatibility with Quollix, run a service powered by Quollix, or offer hosting, consulting, support, or managed services for Quollix.

Commercial use of Quollix source code is allowed under the terms of its open source license.

If you run an unmodified Quollix user interface for your own use or organization, you may keep the included Quollix name, logo, and branding.

You may state that your project is a fork of Quollix or is based on Quollix, provided that this is accurate and does not imply official status.

If you modify Quollix, fork it, or offer Quollix as a hosted or managed service, users must be able to clearly distinguish your offering from the official Quollix project. Use your own product name, logo, and visual identity unless you have prior written permission.

## Prohibited Use

You may not use the Quollix name, logo, or branding in a way that implies ownership, official affiliation, or endorsement when that is not true.

You do not acquire ownership of the Quollix name, logo, website branding, or other branding assets by running, hosting, modifying, or distributing Quollix.
